About My Company

We love creating new digital experiences or improving existing ones. Digital design, user experience design, web design, interaction design and usability testing services are all parts of serving our clients online presence and we have decades of experience passionately pleasing clients. We love solving online shopping problems.

My team and I (there are only two of us) have been published internationally as doing this work since 1997, when we helped avoid buyer's remorse by offering the first website in history that streamed videos of video games, online, at dial-up Internet speeds, so video gamers could view before they bought. We love solving problems for online shoppers. It comes down to experience, loving what we do, and working hard to do it so well, we make it sound easy: but it's not!
