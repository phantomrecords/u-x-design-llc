About My Company

"U. X. Design", also written as, "DBA U. X. Design", is fresh and innovative application of UX / UI / and Mutli-media design thinking. I specialize in what I call "digital experience design", also known as U X Design and U I Design (not C X Design, which is Customer Experience Design). What I do is still "fresh" today because the field of "user experience design" changes by nature. I have been doing this since before I picked up a glass of wine at a bar and before my first time ever getting drunk. I went out to drink after spending 120 hours spread out across seven days and my friends staged an intervention and took me drinking. I was too serious back then. These days, I'm only serious about my health and living my best life. Everything life has thrown at me I've been lucky to get (if it's the good stuff) or lucky to dodge (if it's the bad stuff). When you get to my age you learn life is just luck. Dumb luck. If you do business with me, you'll know I'm a workaholic. If you don't, you probably will never know me. I'm not big on attention. It's been unlucky for me. Now to finish what I was building up to in the first three sentences: that my company is a Frontend Web Development company. Some truncate that to Frontend Development. I specialize in web-based work. So for me it is Frontend Web Development.
